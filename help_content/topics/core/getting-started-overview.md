Welcome to Spwig! This guide gives you a quick tour of your admin dashboard and walks you through the essential steps to launch your online store.

## The Dashboard

After logging in, you land on the **Shop Dashboard** — your control center for monitoring store performance.

![Admin dashboard](/static/core/admin/img/help/getting-started-overview/admin-dashboard.webp)

The dashboard shows:
- **Actions Required** — pending orders, low stock alerts, abandoned carts, and available updates
- **Sales Revenue** — revenue and order charts grouped by day, week, or month
- **Quick Navigation** — jump to any section from the dropdown

## Admin Sidebar

The sidebar on the left organizes everything into sections:

| Section | What's Inside |
|---------|--------------|
| **Products** | Product catalog, categories, subscription plans, gift cards |
| **Orders** | Order management, carts, shipments, shipping settings, tax rates |
| **Customers** | Customer profiles, analytics, LTV settings |
| **Search** | Search analytics, synonyms, redirects |
| **Marketing** | Promotions, vouchers, affiliates, loyalty, blog, announcements |
| **Settings** | Store settings, payments, design, email, translations, POS |

Click the **hamburger icon** (☰) at the bottom to collapse the sidebar for more screen space.

## First Steps

### 1. Configure Store Settings

Go to **Settings > Store Settings** to set up your store identity:
- Store name and tagline
- Site URL
- Favicon and logo
- Default language and timezone

See [Configuring Store Settings](#) for details.

### 2. Add Your Products

Navigate to **Products > All Products** and click **+ Add Product**:
- Enter product name, description, and SKU
- Upload images via the Media Library
- Set pricing and sale options
- Configure inventory tracking

See [Adding a Product](#) for a full walkthrough.

### 3. Set Up Payments

Go to **Settings > Payment Dashboard** to connect a payment provider:
- Browse available providers (Stripe, PayPal, and more)
- Follow the setup wizard to enter your API credentials
- Test the connection before going live

### 4. Configure Shipping

Visit **Orders > Shipments** to set up delivery options:
- Create shipping methods with fixed rates
- Define shipping zones by country or region
- Optionally connect carrier integrations for real-time rates

See [Setting Up Shipping](#) for details.

### 5. Customize Your Design

Navigate to **Settings > Design & Theme** to personalize your store:
- Choose your active theme
- Customize branding (colors, typography, spacing)
- Build your header and footer with the drag-and-drop builders
- Configure navigation menus

See [Design & Themes](#) for details.

### 6. Go Live

When everything is ready:
1. Set your products to **Published** status
2. Verify your store URL in Store Settings
3. Test the checkout flow by placing a test order
4. Ensure your site status shows **Site Active**

## Tips

- Use the **Help** button in the top-right corner of any page to get contextual help for the page you're on.
- The dashboard's **Actions Required** cards link directly to the items that need attention — click them to jump straight there.
- Bookmark the dashboard as your daily starting point to keep track of store health.

Product reviews allow customers to rate and write about their experience with a product. Reviews that are approved by you appear on the product page in your storefront, where they help other customers make purchase decisions. Spwig gives you full control over which reviews are visible — nothing is published until you approve it.

Navigate to **Catalog > Product Reviews** to manage all reviews.

## The review list

The review list gives you an overview of all reviews submitted across your store:

| Column | Description |
|---|---|
| **Product** | The product being reviewed |
| **User** | The customer who submitted the review |
| **Rating** | Star rating from 1 to 5 |
| **Approved** | Whether the review is currently visible on the storefront |
| **Verified Purchase** | Whether the reviewer actually purchased the product |
| **Created At** | When the review was submitted |

### Filtering reviews

Use the filters on the right to focus on specific review states:

- **Rating** — show only reviews with a specific star rating (useful for investigating 1-star complaints)
- **Is Approved** — quickly separate approved from pending reviews
- **Is Verified Purchase** — filter to see only reviews from confirmed buyers

Use the search field to find reviews by product name, customer username, or review title.

## Approving and rejecting reviews

Reviews are not visible on your storefront until approved. You can approve or reject reviews individually or in bulk.

### Bulk actions

1. In the review list, tick the checkboxes next to the reviews you want to act on
2. Select **Approve selected reviews** or **Reject selected reviews** from the **Action** dropdown
3. Click **Go**

This is the most efficient way to process a batch of new reviews.

### Individual review

1. Click a review row to open the detail view
2. Check or uncheck the **Is Approved** field
3. Click **Save**

## What a review contains

When you open an individual review you can see all the detail a customer submitted:

- **Title** — the headline the customer gave their review
- **Comment** — the full written review text
- **Rating** — the star rating (1–5)
- **Is Verified Purchase** — set automatically when the reviewing customer has a completed order for that product
- **Helpful Count** — how many times other customers have marked this review as helpful
- **Images** — any photos the customer attached to their review

You cannot edit the content of a review — you can only approve or reject it.

## Reviewing ratings at a glance

The review list is sorted with the most recent reviews first, making it easy to stay on top of newly submitted feedback. If you want to spot patterns quickly:

- **Filter by Rating = 1** to see all critical reviews in one list — these often reveal product issues or fulfilment problems worth addressing
- **Filter by Is Approved = No** to see your moderation queue — clear this regularly so customers don't wait too long for their reviews to appear
- **Filter by Is Verified Purchase = Yes** to see only reviews from actual buyers, which tend to be the most credible for other customers

## Tips

- Set a regular schedule to check the approval queue — customers notice when their review takes weeks to appear and it can discourage future feedback
- Approving reviews promptly, including critical ones, builds trust with your customers; visible negative reviews paired with no response can look worse than a handled complaint
- Use the **Verified Purchase** filter when deciding how to handle borderline reviews — feedback from confirmed buyers carries more weight in any dispute
- A product with many 1-star reviews that all mention the same issue is a clear signal to investigate packaging, product quality, or your product listing
- Reviews cannot be edited, so if a review contains inappropriate content your only option is to reject it — be consistent in applying your moderation standards

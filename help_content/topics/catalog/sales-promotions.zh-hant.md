銷售與促銷讓您可以建立批量折扣活動，這些活動會自動套用到目錄中的所有產品。請在管理側邊欄中導航至 **Marketing > Sales & Promotions**。

![Promotion list](/static/core/admin/img/help/sales-promotions/promotion-list.webp)

## Promotions Dashboard

The promotions page shows an overview with:

- **Stats Cards** — Total Promotions, Active Now, Scheduled, and Expired counts at a glance
- **Quick Actions** — One-click button to create a new promotion
- **Filters** — Search by name, filter by Status, Discount Type, and target scope
- **Promotion Cards** — Each promotion displayed with full details

## Creating a Promotion

1. Click **+ Create Promotion** in the top right
2. Fill in the promotion details:
   - **Name** — Descriptive title (e.g., "Black Friday Sale")
   - **Discount Type** — Choose from Percentage Off, Amount Off, or Fixed Sale Price
   - **Discount Value** — The discount amount or percentage
   - **Priority** — Higher numbers take precedence when multiple promotions overlap
3. Set the **target scope**:
   - **All Products** — Applies to your entire catalog
   - **Specific Categories** — Only products in selected categories
   - **Specific Brands** — Only products from selected brands
   - **Specific Collections** — Only products in selected collections
   - **Specific Products** — Hand-pick individual products
4. Configure the **schedule**:
   - **Start Date** — When the promotion becomes active
   - **End Date** — When the promotion expires
5. Click **Save**

## Discount Types

| Type | How It Works | Example |
|------|-------------|---------|
| **Percentage Off** | Reduces price by a percentage | 20% OFF — A $100 item becomes $80 |
| **Amount Off** | Reduces price by a fixed amount | $10 OFF — A $100 item becomes $90 |
| **Fixed Sale Price** | Sets a specific sale price | Fixed $49.99 — Item sells at $49.99 regardless of original price |

## Managing Promotions

### Promotion Cards

Each promotion card shows:
- **Name** and discount summary (e.g., "20.00% OFF — Percentage Off")
- **Status badge** — Active or Inactive
- **Target scope** — What products are affected
- **Product count** — How many products are included
- **Priority level** — For resolving overlapping promotions
- **Date range** — Start and end dates
- **Last updated** — When the promotion was last modified

### Promotion Actions

Each card has action buttons:
- **Edit** — Modify the promotion settings
- **Activate/Deactivate** — Toggle the promotion on or off
- **Delete** — Remove the promotion permanently

### Filtering Promotions

Use the filter bar to narrow down your list:
- **Search** — Find promotions by name
- **Status** — Active, Scheduled, Expired, or Inactive
- **Discount Type** — Percentage Off, Amount Off, or Fixed Price
- **Applies To** — Filter by target scope

## Priority System

When multiple active promotions apply to the same product, the promotion with the **highest priority number** takes precedence. For example:
- "Black Friday Sale" (Priority 10, 20% off) vs "Christmas Sale" (Priority 5, 30% off)
- The Black Friday Sale applies because it has a higher priority

Set priority strategically to control which discounts customers see.

## Tips

- Schedule promotions in advance so they activate and expire automatically.
- Use high priority values for your most important campaigns.
- Start with "All Products" promotions for store-wide sales, and use category or brand targeting for focused campaigns.
- Monitor the Active Now and Expired counts on the dashboard to track campaign status.
- Combine promotions with announcements to maximize visibility on your storefront.

title: Announcements
---

Announcements let you display promotional banners and notices on your storefront. They appear in your store's header or footer announcement bar to highlight sales, important updates, or special offers. Navigate to **Marketing > Announcements** in the admin sidebar.

![Announcement list](/static/core/admin/img/help/announcements/announcement-list.webp)

## Announcement List

The announcement page shows all your announcements with:

- **Filter Tabs** — Quick filter by All, Enabled, Disabled, or Expired
- **Search** — Find announcements by text
- **Announcement Cards** — Each announcement with status and configuration details

## Creating an Announcement

1. Click **+ Add Announcement** in the top right
2. Fill in the announcement details:
   - **Message** — The text customers will see (supports translations for multi-language stores)
   - **Priority** — Controls display order when multiple announcements are active
   - **Link Type** — Choose what happens when customers click the announcement:
     - **No Link** — Announcement is informational only
     - **Product** — Links to a specific product page
     - **Category** — Links to a category page
     - **Custom URL** — Links to any URL
3. Configure display settings:
   - **Enabled** — Toggle the announcement on or off
   - **Direct Link** — Whether clicking goes directly to the target
   - **Background Color** — Custom banner background color
   - **Text Color** — Custom text color
   - **Image** — Optional background or decorative image
4. Set scheduling (optional):
   - **Start Date** — When the announcement starts displaying
   - **End Date** — When the announcement expires
5. Click **Save**

## Announcement Cards

Each announcement card displays:
- **Message text** — The announcement content
- **Priority level** — For ordering multiple announcements
- **Link Type** — No Link, Product, Category, or Custom URL
- **Link behavior** — Direct Link or other action
- **Status badges** — Enabled/Disabled, expiry status, image indicator
- **Last updated** — When it was last modified
- **Edit button** — Quick access to modify the announcement

## Multiple Announcements

When multiple announcements are active:
- They **rotate automatically** in the announcement bar on the storefront
- **Priority** controls the display order — higher priority shows first
- Customers can dismiss individual announcements if the header widget is configured to allow it

## Announcement in the Header

Announcements display through the **Announcement Bar widget** in the Header Builder:

1. Navigate to **Settings > Design & Theme > Header Builder**
2. Add the **Announcement Bar** widget to your header
3. Configure the widget settings:
   - **Auto-rotate speed** — Time between announcement transitions
   - **Show close button** — Whether customers can dismiss it
   - **Animation style** — Slide, fade, or static display

## Tips

- Keep announcement text short and actionable — "Free shipping on orders over $50!" is better than a long description.
- Use announcements for time-sensitive promotions, not permanent information.
- Set expiry dates so expired promotions disappear automatically.
- Match announcement colors to your store's branding for a professional look.
- Use the Product link type to drive traffic to specific items you want to promote.
- Limit active announcements to 2-3 at a time to avoid overwhelming customers.
